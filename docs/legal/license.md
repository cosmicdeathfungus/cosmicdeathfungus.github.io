## Why CC BY-SA 4.0?

This is the same license that protects **Wikipedia**. It was chosen
deliberately:

- **Attribution** keeps the authorship and the scientific citation chain
  intact.
- **ShareAlike** is *viral*: every derivative must carry the same license. This
  means the community can freely fork, translate, and expand the protocol — but
  **no one can ever take it, close it, and sell it as a proprietary product.**

This is structurally correct for a community health protocol: maximally open,
permanently protected.
