## What Is This?

This site is the **canonical, openly-licensed, version-controlled** home for the
**CDF NAC Protocol** — a community-developed approach to addressing systemic
fungal and *Candida*-related issues, originally authored by **Peribsen &
MacLir**.

It exists so the information is **permanent and forkable**: indexed by search
engines, maintainable by the community through pull requests, and resilient
against removal from any single platform.

## Why a Website and Not Just a Forum Post?

Forum posts disappear. Threads get locked, accounts get suspended, platforms
change their rules. This documentation is:

- **Search-indexed** — findable by people who don't know the community exists.
- **Version-controlled** — every change is tracked; nothing is silently lost.
- **Openly licensed** — [CC BY-SA 4.0](legal/license.md); free to share and
  adapt, impossible to lock away.
- **Community-maintained** — anyone can [contribute](community/contributing.md)
  corrections, sources, and translations through pull requests.
